Partial repipe

Replace all horizontal pipes from water meter and connect to all vertical pipes leading to the upper floors. Replace all pipes to water heater and all basement fixtures. Replace all shutoffs and replace all silcocks(hose spigots).

Complete repipe

Most older homes have water pipes made out of galvanized iron which, over time, form rust on the inside through a process called tuberculation.
As your pipes build up more rust, your water pressure decreases and your pipes eventually start to leak. Solve this problem by replacing all of your old water pipes with new copper pipes which are designed to last 50 years in your home, almost twice the lifespan of galvanized pipes.
